Information You Provide to Us

Account information:

ILights uses “Firebase Auth” for authentication and you have to ways to sign up.

If you choose ”Sign in with Google” we will store email, name associate with your Google Account. This username will be stored on Firebase Realtime Database system. 

If you choose to “Sign Up” then you will need to provide an email/username, name and password to authenticate yourself to use the app. The provided name may not be your real name and you can also choose any email which may or may not exist. However if the account creation is successful using the email you provided and the email does not exist then you will not be able to verify your account or use the “forgot password feature”, as Firebase will send you an email containing  “verification link ” and “password reset link ” to your email’s inbox. We don’t store your provided password anywhere as auth process is completely managed by “Firebase Auth system”.

The app also uses an internal role-based access for it’s users to stop any suspicious activity. When you first sign up you have a default first level access and you can interact with the app instantly after signing up. The Users with higher access level can change your access level without any prior notice if any suspicious activity is detected. Also, the users with admin level access and higher can see your email and name which you provided during the signup. 

You can delete your account from the settings page and all your data will be deleted.

 

FYI: There are three levels of users: 

  • User (Level 1)
  • Admin (Level 2)
  • Admin (Level 3)

CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

Under the CCPA, among other rights, California consumers have the right to:

  • Request that a business that collects a consumer's personal data disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data that a business has collected about consumers.
  • Request that a business delete any personal data about the consumer that a business has collected.
  • Request that a business that sells a consumer's personal data, not sell the consumer's personal data.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

 

GDPR Data Protection Rights

  • The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

  • The right to data portability – You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
